About Me

Hello! I'm Kyle Scidmore, currently developing my skills in Geographic Information Systems through coursework at Mount Royal University. This portfolio showcases some of the projects I've completed, demonstrating my ability to apply GIS techniques to real-world scenarios.

Through this course, I've gained experience with key industry tools like ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, StoryMaps, and Experience Builder, focusing on areas such as hydrological analysis, spatial modeling, image classification, and effective data visualization.

I'm passionate about using spatial data to understand and address challenges in environmental restoration and resource management.
